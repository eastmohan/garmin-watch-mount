# garmin-watch-mount
**What** <br>
This is a simple way to mount your Garmin Venu SQ on the handlebars of your bike so you can easily view ride statistics and notifications while riding without taking your hand off the bars to look at your wrist.<br>
<br>**Why**<br>
My watch strap is falling apart, but the watch still works pretty well. Rather than spend $6 on a new strap, I decided to make this. Once the strap actually breaks, I'll get a new strap for running, but this should be nice for viewing ride statistics and notifications on your handlebars without any safety risk or spending an extra $200+ on a dedicated bike computer.<br>
<br>**How**<br>
I made this in Fusion 360. It uses the pins that the Garmin straps are attached to to keep the watch in place. I may add a TPU version where just that part is TPU to make it hold better.<br>
<br>**Parts:**<br>
- top.stl
- bottom.stl
- mount (no attachment arm).stl *only if you don't already have a Garmin (or similar) bike mount - I will add a version with a proper way to attatch it to the handlebars eventually.*
- 4 M3*25 screws
- 4* M3 nuts
